Nigerian Jollof

Nigerian Jollof Rice is a rich and incredibly tasty West African Meal. It is a very versatile dish, and it is usually made from scratch using Rice, Tomatoes, Pimento peppers, Tomato paste, Scotch bonnet, Onions, Salt, and other spices.It is one of the most common West African dishes eaten in the regions of Nigeria, Ghana, Togo, Senegal, Gambia, and Mali. However, each of these West African countries has their own variation of this meal. It is inexpensive and easy to make yet, delicious.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

35 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : chicken lettuce corn beef carrot

instructions

step 1

Start by sauteeing the Onions. After about 3 to 5 minutes, stir in the Tomato paste.

step 2

Add the blended peppers and leave for about 5 minutes. Thereafter, add the Thyme, Curry powder, Salt, crayfish and seasoning cubes. Let the mixture simmer well for about 10 minutes.

step 3

Next, stir in the rinsed Rice properly until you cover each grain with the sauce. Now add the chicken stock. Give it a brief stir and cover it up with a tight-fitting lid.

ingredients

6 Cups Rice

3/4 cup oil

Blended pepper sauce

1 large tomato

5 Tbsp Tomato paste

1 medium onion

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
